My Profile

Jeremy Chambers, also known by the sobriquet "Jerry", is the first child of Reverend Major Canute and Marlette Chambers. During his formative years, he migrated to the United Kingdom where he acquired a wonderful palette of experiences before returning to his beautiful homeland, Jamaica.

Reverend Chambers holds a Doctorate in Educational Leadership and Technology (EdD) from the Boyer School of Education, a MSc in Marketing and Global Communications from the University of Liverpool in the United Kingdom, a Post Graduate Certificate in Education (PGCE) from the University of Plymouth in the United Kingdom as well as a Business Degree from the University of Technology, Jamaica.

He became the first Jamaican to ever receive the 'Student of the Year' award at the University of Liverpool and is also the school's most improved student. In 2022, he was sworn as a Reverend for the Anglican Diocese of Jamaica and the Cayman Islands. In the same year, he was honored as a Fellow of the Chartered Management Institute. In 2024, Chambers was made a Fellow of the Fédération Internationale d'Education Physique (FIEP).
