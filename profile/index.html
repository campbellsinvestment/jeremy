My Profile

Dr. Jeremy Chambers is a Chartered Manager Fellow and Assessor with the Chartered Management Institute. He holds a Doctorate in Educational Leadership and Technology from the Boyer School of Education and a Master of Science in Marketing and Global Communications from the University of Liverpool, U.K. His research focuses on andragogy, gerontology, and marketing to the elderly.

Jeremy has written numerous academic papers through the University of Plymouth, University of Liverpool, ADVAN College of Languages, and the University of Technology, Jamaica. His work has been published in leading journals such as Educational Review USA, Journal of Advances in Education Research, Creative Education Journal, International Journal of Adult Education and Technology, US-China Education Review, The International Academic Forum Conference Proceedings, and publications of the National Research Institute of Japan. He is the author of The Emerging Gray Market in ESL as well as the Marketing Musings volumes, and he is currently completing his fourth book, to be published by iUniverse. In addition, he serves as a Senior Reviewer for the Educational Research Journal and the International Academic Forum, and sits on the board of governors for the Council of Continuous Education Futures (CCEF3) International Conference, which unites education practitioners worldwide. He also contributes as an adjudicator for the Japan Forensic League.

Alongside his academic career, Dr. Chambers has consulted for global brands including Toyota, Suzuki, Mochida Pharmaceuticals, and Nihon Plast. He also works extensively in leadership development, preparing managers from Babcock, Princess Yachts, and the National Health Service (NHS) for senior management roles. He currently works with Mindsmiththinking as a Senior Associate.

With more than eighteen years of teaching and coaching experience, Dr. Chambers has taught at high school, college, and university levels across the United States, Jamaica, South Korea, Japan, and the United Kingdom. He earned a Postgraduate Certificate in Education from the University of Plymouth, becoming a trained teacher with qualified teaching and learning status (QTLS).

Beyond his academic and professional pursuits, Dr. Chambers is passionate about both marketing and education. He is also an avid sports enthusiast with a love of sumo, an active practitioner of judo, and a committed powerlifter who enjoys training with friends.
